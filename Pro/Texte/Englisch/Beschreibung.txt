Powerkey is hard to press?
No Problem! - Here is ScreenLock!
ScreenLock. Lock your phone with style!

This is a Widget which locks your screen by tapping on it. But with this widget you can also lock and unlock the screen by simply shaking the device! So if your powerkey on the device is broken, it doesn't work properly or you just don't want your powerkey to wear off, this is the right thing fo you!

Try the free 7-days trial version of ScreenLock named "ScreenLock Light".

Caution!
Before you can uninstall the widget, you have to go to: "Settings->Security->Select device administrators->ScreenLock Pro" and click "Deactivate".
